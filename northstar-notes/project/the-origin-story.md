## The Beginning: A Simple Need

**The Problem**: British Centre Burgas (Zlatina's private language school) has been renting several floors in a privately owned building for 28 years. They want their own building.

**The Question**: "How do we find funding to buy a building?"

**The Search Begins**: Hours spent on Google, grants.gov, fundsforngos.org, candid.org, foundation websites, EU portals...

**The Realization**: "This is incredibly time-consuming, overwhelming, and inefficient. If it's this hard for us, it must be this hard for thousands of other organizations."

**The Decision**: Build a platform that automates funding discovery.

## The Catalyst: "How Do We Find Funding?"

### The Manual Process (Before NorthStar Funding Discovery):
1. Google search: "education grants Bulgaria"
2. Visit 50+ websites: foundations, government portals, EU programs
3. Read through eligibility criteria (often in English, sometimes in other languages)
4. Track deadlines manually (spreadsheets, calendars)
5. Repeat weekly because opportunities change constantly
6. **Time Investment**: 20-40 hours per week
7. **Success Rate**: Low (miss opportunities, unclear eligibility, overwhelmed by volume)

## Why This Story Matters for Platform Design

### 1. For-Profit Education Is Not an Edge Case
- British Centre Burgas = the original use case
- For-profit funding sources are CRITICAL, not optional
- RecipientOrganizationType must include FOR_PROFIT categories

### 2. Building Purchase Is a Real, Specific Need
- Not abstract "educational infrastructure"
- Specific amount range (‚Ç¨200k-500k)
- Specific funding mechanisms (loans, private investment)
- ProjectScale taxonomy must support this

### 3. NGO Scholarship Funding Is Multi-Dimensional
- Scholarships (tuition + Cambridge exams)
- Talent scouts (salaries for outreach)
- Future building (long-term infrastructure)
- BeneficiaryPopulation must include DISADVANTAGED_CHILDREN, LOW_INCOME

### 4. Bulgarian Language Is Not Optional
- Zlatina's primary language is Bulgarian
- Search must support Bulgarian queries
- Results must be presentable in Bulgarian
- QueryLanguage enum is immediate need, not future nice-to-have

### 5. Real People Are Waiting for This
- Not a hypothetical market
- Zlatina needs this NOW (building purchase)
- NorthStar Foundation needs this NOW (scholarship funding)
- Huw validates we're building the right thing (15+ years grant expertise)
